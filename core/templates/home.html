Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of this club?
Our club aims to foster practical skills, innovation, and collaboration in areas like cybersecurity, programming, hardware, design, and digital technologies. We help students go beyond academics and become industry-ready.
Who can join the club?
Any student of our institute who is curious about technology and eager to learn is welcome! No prior experience is needed.
How do I join the club?
You can register using our registration form during open enrollment periods. After that, you'll be added to our communication channels.
Will I get mentorship or training?
Yes! Each wing has experienced mentors and seniors who will guide you through structured learning paths, projects, and hands-on sessions.
Is there any fee to join the club?
Yes a small fee is required, we will try to keep it as low as possible.
